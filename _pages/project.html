Research Project (Publications and Competitions)

Publications

Combination of Adversarial and Likelihood Learning for Image Restoration
Advisor: Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang, Postdoc Xiangyu Xu
1. Analyzed crucial flaws of Flows for Image Reconstruction with weak generalization of naturally degraded images.
2. Combined Regularized Likelihood Maximization and Adversarial Learning to generalize into real-world images.
3. Conducted experiments on SR, Impainting and Colorization tasks and analyzed the advantages within our model.

Competitions

NTIRE2020 Smartphone Image Super Resolution
Advisor: Asso. Prof. Tingting Jiang

Advisor: Asso. Prof. Tingting Jiang
